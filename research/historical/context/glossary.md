# Glossary of Terms and Acronyms

Quick reference for interpreting declassified documents.

---

## US Government Agencies

| Acronym | Full Name | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **CIA** | Central Intelligence Agency | Foreign intelligence, covert operations |
| **DCI** | Director of Central Intelligence | Head of CIA |
| **DDO** | Deputy Director for Operations | Heads clandestine service |
| **DDP** | Deputy Director for Plans | Pre-1973 name for DDO |
| **DIA** | Defense Intelligence Agency | Military intelligence |
| **FBI** | Federal Bureau of Investigation | Domestic law enforcement |
| **INR** | Bureau of Intelligence and Research | State Dept intelligence analysis |
| **NSA** | National Security Agency | Signals intelligence |
| **NSC** | National Security Council | Presidential advisory body |
| **ARA** | Bureau of Inter-American Affairs | State Dept Latin America bureau |

## Key Terms
