# Contextual Research

Background knowledge required to interpret the declassified documents.

**This is separate from document analysis** - see `../RESEARCH_PLAN.md` for document-focused research.

## Purpose

Build the contextual knowledge needed to properly interpret CIA documents:
- Who produced these documents and why
- Historical events referenced
- Key actors and institutions
- Secondary literature interpretations
