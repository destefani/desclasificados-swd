## Notes on Sources

### Reliability Hierarchy

1. **Primary sources**: Declassified documents, official reports
2. **National Security Archive**: Curated, annotated document collections
3. **Scholarly books**: Peer-reviewed, footnoted
4. **Wikipedia**: Useful for overview, always cross-reference facts
5. **News sources**: Contemporary accounts, may have biases
